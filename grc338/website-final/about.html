About Us

Welcome to July Sips, your cozy neighborhood café where every sip tells a story. We’re passionate about crafting freshly brewed coffee, smooth espresso drinks, and a variety of hand-selected teas. Our menu also features freshly baked pastries and hearty breakfast options made with care and the finest ingredients.

At July Sips, we believe that a café is more than just a place to grab a drink—it’s a space to pause, connect, and enjoy life’s simple pleasures. Whether you’re starting your morning with a latte, meeting friends over a muffin, or taking a quiet moment to read, our goal is to make every visit special.

We take pride in creating a welcoming, warm environment where everyone feels at home. From locally sourced ingredients to thoughtful preparation, every detail is designed to bring comfort, flavor, and a little bit of joy to your day. At July Sips, it’s all about community, quality, and savoring the little moments—one sip at a time.
